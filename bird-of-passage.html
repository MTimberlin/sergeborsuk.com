Bird of Passage

Ivan Kurbakov  ·  #Feature #Full-Length

#Feature #Full-Length

A young actress travels through Georgia in search of a better life. Her journey takes her from the noisy feasts of Tbilisi, with endless songs and conversations, to the silence of the overgrown ruins of Tskaltubo spa resort.

On an empty stage, she performs a play with ghosts. Sleeping dogs dream of the end of history.

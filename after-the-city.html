After The City

Directed By Igor Makarov

#Architecture #Documentary

A four-part documentary series about architecture in Saint Petersburg, directed by Igor Makarov. The city from a few different angles: through architect Sergey Tchoban, a family living in a small apartment in a modern building, people from the historic center, and folks living on a houseboat.

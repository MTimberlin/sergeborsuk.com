After The City

By Igor Makarov

A four-part documentary series about architecture in Saint Petersburg. You see the city from a few different angles: through architect Sergey Tchoban, a family living in a small apartment in a modern building, people from the historic center, and folks living on a houseboat.

The Girl In The Sands

By Serge Borsuk

A minimalist documentary shot in Kyrgyzstan on the southern shore of Lake Issyk-Kul. A sandy area scorched by the sun and blown by winds. There is only one house standing there.

Marked Wings

By Tamara Pronina

Ornithologist Igor Koryakin and his family go to India to figure out why steppe eagles are dying. It’s about how human activity is affecting these rare birds of prey.
